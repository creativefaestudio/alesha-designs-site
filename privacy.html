Privacy

Privacy Notice

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Information collected

Alesha Designs may collect your name, email address, business or organization name, project details, links, uploaded files, desired deadline, and payment-related confirmation details needed to complete a visual audit service.

How information is used

Information is used to review submitted materials, complete the purchased audit, communicate about the project, maintain business records, and improve the client intake process.

Client materials

Client materials may include websites, screenshots, PDFs, campaign files, social graphics, brand guides, Canva links, Google Drive links, or other visual assets. Do not submit passwords, private login credentials, protected personal data, medical data, financial account data, or materials you do not have permission to share.

Payment processing

Payments should be processed through a secure payment provider such as Stripe. Alesha Designs should not collect full card numbers directly through this website.

File storage

Submitted materials may be stored in business file storage such as Google Drive or another secure workspace used to complete the audit. Access should be limited to the person completing the service and any authorized support providers required for operations.

Third-party tools

The service may use tools such as Fillout, Stripe, Google Workspace, GitHub Pages, email services, or other operational platforms. Each third-party tool has its own terms and privacy practices.

For privacy-related questions, email hello@aleshadesigns.com.
