Service Terms

Last updated: May 13, 2026

Written audit service

Alesha Designs provides written visual audit and design QA services. Standard audit purchases do not include live calls, meetings, unlimited revisions, implementation, redesign, or production work unless specifically included in the purchased service.

Client responsibilities

  • Submit accurate project details and accessible materials.
  • Provide only materials you own or have permission to share.
  • Do not submit passwords or private login credentials.
  • Respond to clarification requests when needed before work begins.

Scope

Each audit is limited to the scope described for the selected service. If the submitted project exceeds the purchased scope, Alesha Designs may recommend a revised service, add-on, or custom scope before work begins.

Feedback format

Audit feedback may include prioritized findings, written notes, annotated observations, and practical recommendations. The service provides visual review and recommendations; it does not guarantee business results, conversion rates, sales, donations, approvals, or audience response.

Turnaround

Turnaround begins after payment is complete and required materials have been received. Rush service may be available as a separate paid add-on depending on current workload.

Calls and follow-up

Live calls are not included with standard audits. Clarification calls, walkthroughs, follow-up passes, and additional review may be offered as separate paid services.

Refunds

Because audits reserve review time and involve custom professional evaluation, refund eligibility should be determined before work begins. Once review work has started, refunds may be limited or unavailable.

Contact

For service questions, email hello@aleshadesigns.com.
