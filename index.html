FAQ

Boundaries are part of the service.

Do I need to book a call?

No. Standard audits are completed through written review. Calls are not included unless purchased separately.

What happens after I start?

You will complete the intake, submit your links or files, and receive the next-step confirmation for your audit.

Can I ask questions after the audit?

Each audit includes the deliverables listed for that service. Additional clarification, follow-up review, or live walkthroughs may be purchased separately.

What if I choose the wrong audit?

Alesha Designs may recommend switching to a better-fit service or adjusting the scope before work begins.

Do you offer rush turnaround?

Rush review may be available as a paid add-on depending on current workload.

Should I send passwords?

No. Do not submit login credentials. Use share links, exported files, screenshots, PDFs, or temporary view-only access when possible.
